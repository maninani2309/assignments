Ingredients

        Everything is sliced length wise
        2 tablespoon vegetable/canola oil
        1 cup Paneer soak cut paneer in warm water for 10 mins , drain , pat dry and use
        ½ cup Sliced peppers
        1 /2 cup sliced onions
        ½ cup steamed peas
        ½ cup steamed sweet corn
        2 to matoes chopped length wise
        3 cloves garlic sliced
        1 tablespoon green chilly and ginger paste
        2 tablespoon chopped coriander
        2 teaspoon red chilly powder – adjust to your spice level
        ½ tablespoon garam masala
        1 tablespoon peanut powder
        1 teaspoon rock salt
        2 teaspoon pudhina powder / 2 tablespoon fresh mint leaves chopped
        Regular Salt to taste
        2 teaspoon kasoori methi
        3-4 tablespoon milk
        Fresh lemon squeezed optional but highly recommended
       
 Instructions

        Heat oil in a skillet on medium.
        Add chilly , ginger paste , garlic, cook for 15 secs.
        Add peppers and onions, cook for a minute.
        Add spices , peanut powder and salt , cook for a minute.
        Add tomatoes and ¼ cup water .
        Give it a quick stir and cook for 2 -3mins or until its tender and oil starts separating
        Add steamed peas and corn
        Add milk followed by paneer .Stir lightly as paneer is quiet soft .
        Finally add kasoori and pudhina powder , coriander and lemon.. cook for a minute …taste test. .
