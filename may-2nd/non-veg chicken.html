Ingredients:

        Chicken (700 gm)
        Ginger Garlic paste - (1 Tbsp)
        Lemon juice (1 Tbsp)
        Salt as per taste
        Red chilli powder - (1/2 Tbsp)
        Edible oil (3 Tbsp)
        Chopped onion - (3 Pcs)
        Ginger Garlic paste - (1/2 Tbsp)
        Red chilli powder - (1 Tbsp)
        Cumin powder - (1/2 Tbsp)
        Garam masala - (1 Tbsp)
        Water - (1 Cup)
        Chopped green chilli- (1 Tbsp)
        Sliced ginger- (1 Tbsp)
        Chopped tomatoes - (3 Pcs)
        Butter - (1 Tbsp)
        Coriander leaves
        
Procedure

        Marinate chicken with ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, salt, and red chilli powder by mixing it well. 
        Allow it to marinate for 15 minutes.
        Heat oil in the pan and saute chopped onion till they are golden brown. 
        Add ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder. cumin powder, garam masala, salt, water and mix it properly.
        Now add chopped green chilli. sliced ginger, chopped tomatoes, marinated chicken, and water to the gravy.
        Mix well and let it cook for 15-20 minutes. Add butter and chicken curry is ready to serve. 
        Garnish with coriander leaves & enjoy the lip- smacking bite.
